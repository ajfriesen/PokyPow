# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I still use my power button?

Yes.

## Do I need an app?

No.

## Do I need Home Assistant?

No. The PokyPow also has a web interface and a REST API.
You can control your PC via the web interace as well.

However, advanced automations will need Home Assistant or another Smart Home Ecosytem.

## How does the PokyPow get power?

When your motherboard has always USB power enabled:

- Over the USB2.0 Header from your Mainboard
- USB-C

or even an external power supply.
