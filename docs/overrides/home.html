How It Works.

Connect power, reset and power LED to the PokyPow.
Connect USB for powering the PokyPow.
Your PC becomes just another switch in your smart home.

  • ‚úì Exposes the Power and Reset buttons to your smart home

# Example ESPHome Config
switch:
  - platform: gpio
    pin: GPIO3
    id: pc_power
    name: "Gaming PC Power"
    on_turn_on:
      - switch.turn_on: pc_power_relay
      - delay: 200ms
      - switch.turn_off: pc_power_relay
