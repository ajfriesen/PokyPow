How It Works.

  1. Flash the PokyPow via the browser.
  2. Connect Power, Reset and the LED from the Case to the PokyPow.
  3. Connect Power, Reset and the LED from the Mainboard to the PokyPow.
  4. Plug into USB2.0 header for power.
  5. Control your PC with Home Assistant!
