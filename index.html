    A board that allows you power on, off, reset, hard reset your PC and check if it is actually running in Home Assistant . You can automate whatever you want.

    Features

    • Power On/Off

      Power on/off your PC with a single click.

    • Reboot/Reset

      Clean reboot/reset your PC.

    • Monitor the Power ON state

      See if and how long your PC has been powered on. Use this for automations to power off your PC after a certain amount of time. Parental control for your gaming kids

    • No Cloud

      No need for a cloud service. Everything is done locally on your network.

    • No App

      No need to install another smart app.

    • Hard Reset

      Your PC has locked up? Do not worry, just hard reset it.

    • / Child Lock/Cat Safe

      Deactivate the power button to prevent your PC from being powered on/off from pets, kids, or other people.

    • Integrates with Home Assistant

      Integrates with Home Assistant to allow for easy control of your PC. Create automations to power on/off your PC at specific time or when you leave the house. The power is yours.

    • API

      Provides a API to allow for easy integration with other services.

    • ESPHome

      Proudly made with ESPHome.
